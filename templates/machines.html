Description: The Zund is an accurate high-speed gantry with a variety of available tools for cutting, plotting, creasing, scoring, milling, drawing, etc. A range of modules provide the capability to cut, score, crease and mill. ShopBot CNC Router

Description: The Abrasive water jet cutter is unlike any conventional machine. It compose of a high pressure water system, accelerating water through a precision nozzle at near sonic speeds. The water carries with it an abrasive particle which chips away the material, thereby cutting it with precision. The water jet is a dynamic tool that can cut complex shapes in a wide range of materials from simple metals to complex composites. It plays an integral part in creating the structure for machines that can be built in the lab. It can be used to cut parts like metal gears, sprockets, machine frame machine parts etc.

Cutting Envelope: 1400 mm x 1400 mm

Z Axis Travel: 200 mm

Table Size: 2000 x 1650 mm

Speed: 4572 mm/min

Linear Positional Accuracy: +/- 0.0254 mm

Repeatability: +/- 0.0254 mm

Applications: Cuts complex flat parts out of most materials including metal, Marble, Granite, Aluminium, titanium, plastic, glass, ceramics and composites up to 6" thick directly
